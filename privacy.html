Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 5, 2026

At Jothi Digital Service, we value the privacy of our visitors and clients. This privacy policy describes the types of information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your personal data when you interact with our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our contact form, we collect the following personal details:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Project message details and requirements

We may also collect non-personal analytics tracking data (such as browser type, referring pages, and duration of visit) to evaluate website performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Respond to contact requests and plan website development projects.

  • Enhance and optimize our website speed and usability.

3. Data Security and Sharing

We employ industry-standard secure socket layers (SSL/HTTPS) to protect transmission data. We never sell, trade, or share your personal information with external third parties.
